About Alumni Connect

Alumni Connect is a platform designed to bridge the gap between current students, alumni, and faculty of the Computer Science Department at Egerton University.

Our mission is to create a vibrant community where knowledge, opportunities, and experiences are shared to foster professional growth and academic excellence.
