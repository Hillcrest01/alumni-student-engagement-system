Frequently Asked Questions

Unable to Receive Account Credentials After Email Submission
Cause:
  • The admin has not yet verified and approved the email address.
  • Email went to the spam/junk folder.
Solution:
  • Wait for the admin to approve your registration.
  • Check your email spam/junk folder.
  • If not received within 48 hours, contact the system administrator for assistance.
Cannot Log In With Temporary Password
Cause:
  • Incorrect entry of email or password.
  • Temporary password has expired or already used.
Solution:
  • Ensure you are entering the correct email address and temporary password as provided in your welcome email.
  • Copy and paste the password directly to avoid typing errors.
  • If expired, request the admin to resend credentials.
System Prompts for Password Change but New Password Is Rejected
Cause:
  • New password does not meet system security requirements (e.g., too short, missing special characters, etc.).
Solution:
  • Create a password that is at least 8 characters long, contains an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special character (e.g., !@#$).
  • Follow the password policy shown on the password change page.
No Alumni Matches Displayed After Profile Completion
Cause:
  • Skills were not entered during profile completion.
  • No alumni profiles with matching skills currently available.
Solution:
  • Edit your profile to ensure your skills are properly listed.
  • Be patient and check back later as more alumni may register over time.
Unable to See My Job/Event Posting on the Platform
Cause:
  • You are a student and your post is pending admin approval.
  • The post may have been rejected by the admin if it did not meet posting guidelines.
Solution:
  • Wait for admin approval (you will receive a notification once approved).
  • If your post was rejected, review the reason provided and resubmit with corrections.
Not Receiving Replies From Alumni After Initiating a Conversation
Cause:
  • Alumni may not have seen the message yet.
  • Alumni may not be actively using the platform.
Solution:
  • Be patient and allow time for alumni to respond.
  • Consider reaching out to other available alumni matches for better engagement.
Forgot Password and Cannot Log In
Cause:
  • User has forgotten their login password.
Solution:
  • Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.
  • Follow the password reset instructions sent to your registered email.
Unable to View Departmental Announcements
Cause:
  • Network issues or session timeout.
  • System maintenance in progress.
Solution:
  • Refresh the page or log out and log back in.
  • If the issue persists, try accessing the system later or contact system support.
